Our Services

Discover our comprehensive range of services related to PCB solutions

Single layer PCBs

Conductive material on only one side of the board. Cost-effective for simple electronic circuits.

Double layer PCBs

Conductive material on both sides, with connections through plated holes called vias.

Multi-Layer PCBs

Multiple layers of conductive material with insulating layers in between, offering higher component density.

Flexible PCBs

Built on flexible base material allowing the board to conform to a desired shape or flex during use.

PCB Stencil

A thin metal sheet used to apply paste on the board so components can stick in the right place before soldering.

CAD/CAM solutions

Software tools that help design and prepare circuit boards for manufacturing quickly and accurately.
